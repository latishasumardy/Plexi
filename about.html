About Plexi

Plexi is a mental and emotional health website where users can go to find quick and easy relief from negative emotions (specifically anxiety, depression, insecurity, and anger). These emotions can be relieved through a variety of media such as pictures, games, and music that are intended to distract users and lighten their emotions. While the makers of Plexi do not claim to be mental health professionals, we sourced all of our tips and links from trustworthy websites and we hope our site is useful and helpful.
